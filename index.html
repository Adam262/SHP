About Us

Schoolhouse Partners was founded in 1998 by former members of the Parthenon Group, a strategy consulting firm based in Boston. Conceived as a firm entirely dedicated to creating improvements in public and post-secondary education, Schoolhouse builds enduring partnerships with our clients and portfolio companies. We help these organizations deliver better services to students and increased value to stakeholders.

Strategic Advisory Services

Strategy is a set of choices – usually few in number – that differentiates an organization from its competitors in the minds of its customers. As a strategic advisor, Schoolhouse Partners helps its clients identify and make these choices in ways that generate sustainable performance and market leadership.
